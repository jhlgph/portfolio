Info Graphics - South Korea

Class: Graphic Design 3

Date: November 2024

Program: Adobe Illustrator

Size: 8x8 inches

Description: This project explores data info about South Korea that includes the following: historical timeline, population growth over a 100 year timespan, population comparison to five nearby countries, geography with major cities and attractions, land comparison to five nearby countries, eight agricultural products, seasons/weather, and top three imports/exports. In an engaging illustrative booklet.
